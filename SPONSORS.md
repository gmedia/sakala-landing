# Sponsors

Sakala is an open-source project supported by organizations and individuals who help provide infrastructure, development resources, community support, and long-term sustainability.

## Founding Sponsor

### GMEDIA / PT Media Sarana Data

GMEDIA is the founding sponsor and initial infrastructure supporter of Sakala.

GMEDIA provides early infrastructure, domain support, development environment, and technical support to help Sakala reach its MVP and early pilot stage.

This sponsorship does not change Sakala's open-source principles. Sakala remains developed through open documentation, transparent roadmap, public issues, and community contribution.

## Future Sponsors

Sakala may accept additional sponsors in the future to support infrastructure, documentation, community programs, education pilots, and long-term maintenance.
